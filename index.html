Who I Am

My name is Erica Piano, and I am a fullstack web developer in Central New Jersey. I have completed formal education in Web Development, and have expanded skills to creating custom projects from scratch. Currently seeking a role as a front end junior web developer.

What I do

I take the most pride in creating premium content while keeping a clean code. I am able to create a full web application with frontend and server-side functionality. My frontend uses HTML, CSS, JS, and React. I may also use Bootstrap and JQuery to complete frontend functions. I use MySQL and MongoDB to control my queries, and NodeJS to write my backend while incorporating express, sequelize, or mongoose to manage my server-side.
